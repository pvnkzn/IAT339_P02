About Us

An affordable shop selling trendy items.

Cyberdaze is a small store that mainly sells Y2K-style accessories. This includes hats, glasses, jewelry, and more. We keep an eye out for trends and strive to sell products that are popular on social media at an affordable price. We understand it can be intimidating to try a style for the first time, so a good portion of accessories offered are not as flashy and will suit everyday clothing.
